Tribe DAO Docs

Tribe DAO is a community and ecosystem of decentralised finance protocols and products

Get started

Fei Protocol

Fei is a stablecoin pegged to $1 USD. It powers many of the Tribe DAO's services as its liquidity engine.

Turbo

Turbo is a liquidity accelerator that allows any token to become productive through the yield generated off a costless line of Fei credit.

Rari Capital

Permissionless lending and yield with Fuse and Vaults. Rari provides critical financial infrastructure to all tokens in DeFi without any gatekeeping.
